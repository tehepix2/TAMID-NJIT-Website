What Is TAMID?

TAMID bridges the gap between business and technology through hands-on experiences and professional development. We're dedicated to fostering the next generation of business leaders through practical education and real-world experience.

Education at TAMID

Our Educational Approach

We empower students with essential skills for careers in consulting, finance, and technology. Our learning-by-doing approach combines real-world projects with comprehensive education in business strategy, market analysis, and consulting practices.

Workshops

Intensive training sessions on financial modeling, consulting strategies, and leadership skills.

Mentorship

Connect with professionals and alumni in the business world for guidance and networking.

Our Projects

Stock Pitch Project

Members work in teams to select a stock, conduct deep financial analysis, and deliver a professional pitch to potential investors or industry experts.

  • Financial analysis and valuation techniques
  • Public speaking and presentation skills
  • Market research and critical thinking

Consulting Simulation Project

Members collaborate in teams to tackle a business problem presented by a partner company, simulating a real-world consulting project from start to finish.

  • Problem-solving and strategic thinking
  • Client management and presentation
  • Business strategy and research methodologies
